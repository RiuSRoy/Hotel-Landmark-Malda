# Product Overview

Hotel Landmark is a luxury hotel website for a property located in Malda, West Bengal. The site showcases:

- **Premium hotel rooms** (Economy, Deluxe, Suite categories)
- **Banquet hall** for events, weddings, and corporate functions
- **Contact and booking** functionality via phone and WhatsApp
- **Guest reviews** and Google Business Profile integration

## Target Audience
Business travelers and event organizers, with a 70%+ retention rate among business guests.

## Key Business Goals
- Drive direct bookings via phone/WhatsApp
- Showcase luxury accommodations and event spaces
- Maintain strong SEO presence for local searches
- Build trust through reviews and testimonials
